Boston

Brighton Music Hall
158 Brighton Ave, Allston MA 02134
(617) 779-0140 | www.brightonmusichall.com
Capacity: 500. Part of the Bowery Boston family. Good sound. Green Line B to Harvard Ave.

Great Scott
1222 Commonwealth Ave, Allston MA 02134
(617) 566-9014 | www.greatscottboston.com
Capacity: 240. Legendary Allston dive. Intimate room, cheap drinks, sweaty shows. 21+. Green Line B to Harvard Ave.

House of Blues
15 Lansdowne St, Boston MA 02215
(888) 693-2583 | www.houseofblues.com/boston
Capacity: 2,425. Big room near Fenway. Corporate but books good tours. Green Line to Kenmore.

Orpheum Theatre
1 Hamilton Pl, Boston MA 02108
(617) 482-0106 | www.orpheum-theater.com
Capacity: 2,700. Historic theatre from 1852. Seated venue, good acoustics. Red/Green Line to Park Street.

Paradise Rock Club
967 Commonwealth Ave, Boston MA 02215
(617) 562-8800 | www.thedise.com
Capacity: 933. Classic Boston venue since 1977. Great sound, balcony seating available. Green Line B to Pleasant St.

Royale
279 Tremont St, Boston MA 02116
(617) 338-7699 | www.royaleboston.com
Capacity: 1,500. Former theatre, now multi-level club. Green Line to Boylston.

Cambridge / Somerville

Elks Lodge Cambridge
55 Bishop Allen Dr, Cambridge MA 02139
Capacity: 300. DIY all-ages venue. Hardcore matinees on weekends. BYOB. Red Line to Central Square.

Middle East
472-480 Massachusetts Ave, Cambridge MA 02139
(617) 864-3278 | www.mideastoffers.com
Capacity: Downstairs 575, Upstairs 195. The heart of Boston punk/hardcore since 1987. Corner Mall has cheap food. Red Line to Central Square.

The Sinclair
52 Church St, Cambridge MA 02138
(617) 547-5200 | www.sinclaircambridge.com
Capacity: 525. Modern venue in Harvard Square. Good sightlines, restaurant attached. Red Line to Harvard.

Allston / Jamaica Plain / Medford

Allston House Shows
Various locations, Allston MA
DIY basement and living room shows. Check social media for addresses day-of. Bring cash, BYOB, be respectful to neighbors.

Deep Cuts
120 Boston Ave, Medford MA 02155
Capacity: 100. Record store / venue space. Intimate shows, great for local bands. All ages, no booze.

Midway Cafe
3496 Washington St, Jamaica Plain MA 02130
(617) 524-9038 | www.midwaycafe.com
Capacity: 100. Queer-friendly JP dive. Punk, ska, queer dance nights. 21+. Orange Line to Green St.

O'Brien's Pub
3 Harvard Ave, Allston MA 02134
(617) 782-6245
Capacity: 80. Tiny Allston bar with free shows most nights. Local hardcore, metal, punk. 21+ Cash only. Green Line B to Harvard Ave.

Worcester

The Palladium
261 Main St, Worcester MA 01608
(508) 797-9696 | www.thepalladium.net
Capacity: Mainstage 2,200, Upstairs 600. Central MA's biggest metal/hardcore venue. Worth the drive.

Providence RI

AS220
115 Empire St, Providence RI 02903
(401) 831-9327 | www.as220.org
Capacity: 150. Non-profit arts space. DIY shows, all ages, unjuried. The heart of Providence underground.

Fete Music Hall
103 Dike St, Providence RI 02909
(401) 383-1112 | www.fetemusic.com
Capacity: 1,000. Converted ballroom in Olneyville. Good sound, cheap tickets, worth the trip from Boston.

New Hampshire

The Stone Church
5 Granite St, Newmarket NH 03857
(603) 659-7700 | www.stonechurchrocks.com
Capacity: 250. Converted church in small NH town. Great acoustics, all ages, worth the drive.
