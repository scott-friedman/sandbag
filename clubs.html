BOSTON

Brighton Music Hall
158 Brighton Ave, Allston, MA 02134
(617) 779-0140
Capacity: 500 | Ages: 18+ (some a/a)
www.brightonmusichall.com
Part of the Bowery Boston family. Good sound, multiple bars. Street parking or Green Line B to Harvard Ave.

Great Scott
1222 Commonwealth Ave, Allston, MA 02134
(617) 566-9014
Capacity: 240 | Ages: 21+
www.greatscottboston.com
Legendary Allston dive. Intimate room, cheap drinks, sweaty shows. Green Line B to Harvard Ave. RIP original location, long live the new spot.

House of Blues
15 Lansdowne St, Boston, MA 02215
(888) 693-2583
Capacity: 2,425 | Ages: varies
www.houseofblues.com/boston
Big room near Fenway. Corporate but books good tours. Expensive drinks. Green Line to Kenmore.

Middle East
472-480 Massachusetts Ave, Cambridge, MA 02139
(617) 864-3278
Capacity: Downstairs 575, Upstairs 195 | Ages: varies
www.mideastoffers.com
The heart of Boston punk/hardcore since 1987. Downstairs is the main room - loud, hot, legendary. Upstairs is more intimate. Corner Mall has cheap food. Red Line to Central Square.

Orpheum Theatre
1 Hamilton Pl, Boston, MA 02108
(617) 482-0106
Capacity: 2,700 | Ages: a/a
www.orpheum-theater.com
Historic theatre from 1852. Seated venue, good acoustics. Red/Green Line to Park Street.

Paradise Rock Club
967 Commonwealth Ave, Boston, MA 02215
(617) 562-8800
Capacity: 933 | Ages: 18+ (some a/a)
www.thedise.com
Classic Boston venue since 1977. Great sound, balcony seating available. Green Line B to Pleasant St.

Royale
279 Tremont St, Boston, MA 02116
(617) 338-7699
Capacity: 1,500 | Ages: varies
www.royaleboston.com
Former theatre, now multi-level club. Big shows, coat check recommended. Green Line to Boylston.

CAMBRIDGE / SOMERVILLE

Elks Lodge Cambridge
55 Bishop Allen Dr, Cambridge, MA 02139
Capacity: 300 | Ages: a/a
DIY all-ages venue. Hardcore matinees on weekends. BYOB (no alcohol sold). Red Line to Central Square.

The Sinclair
52 Church St, Cambridge, MA 02138
(617) 547-5200
Capacity: 525 | Ages: 18+ (some a/a)
www.sinclaircambridge.com
Modern venue in Harvard Square. Good sightlines, restaurant attached. Red Line to Harvard.

ALLSTON / JAMAICA PLAIN

Allston House Shows
Various locations, Allston, MA
Capacity: varies | Ages: a/a
DIY basement and living room shows. Check social media for addresses day-of. Bring cash, BYOB, be respectful to neighbors. The heart of Boston DIY.

Deep Cuts
120 Boston Ave, Medford, MA 02155
Capacity: 100 | Ages: a/a
Record store / venue space. Intimate shows, great for local bands. All ages, no booze.

Midway Cafe
3496 Washington St, Jamaica Plain, MA 02130
(617) 524-9038
Capacity: 100 | Ages: 21+
www.midwaycafe.com
Queer-friendly JP dive. Punk, ska, queer dance nights. Cheap drinks, good jukebox. Orange Line to Green St.

O'Brien's Pub
3 Harvard Ave, Allston, MA 02134
(617) 782-6245
Capacity: 80 | Ages: 21+
Tiny Allston bar with free shows most nights. Local hardcore, metal, punk. Cash only. Green Line B to Harvard Ave.

WORCESTER

The Palladium
261 Main St, Worcester, MA 01608
(508) 797-9696
Capacity: Mainstage 2,200, Upstairs 600 | Ages: a/a
www.thepalladium.net
Central MA's biggest metal/hardcore venue. Two stages. Parking lot, worth the drive from Boston. Commuter Rail to Worcester.

PROVIDENCE, RI

AS220
115 Empire St, Providence, RI 02903
(401) 831-9327
Capacity: 150 | Ages: a/a
www.as220.org
Non-profit arts space. DIY shows, all ages, unjuried. The heart of Providence underground.

Fete Music Hall
103 Dike St, Providence, RI 02909
(401) 383-1112
Capacity: 1,000 | Ages: varies
www.fetemusic.com
Converted ballroom in Olneyville. Good sound, cheap tickets, worth the trip from Boston.

NEW HAMPSHIRE

The Stone Church
5 Granite St, Newmarket, NH 03857
(603) 659-7700
Capacity: 250 | Ages: a/a
www.stonechurchrocks.com
Converted church in small NH town. Great acoustics, all ages, worth the drive. Local food, good beer.

VENUE TIPS

- Always confirm show details with venue before going
- Bring ID even to all-ages shows (for bar areas)
- Bring cash for merch - bands need it more than Ticketmaster
- Arrive early for GA shows if you want to be up front
- Support the openers - everyone started somewhere
- Don't be a jerk in the pit - pick people up
- Tip your bartenders
- Take the T when possible - parking sucks everywhere
